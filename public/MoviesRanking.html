My Best Movies According to Ash

My top 3 movies of all time.

3 Idiots

Great Movie about student life, hits you with great emotional and funny moments.

Ready Player One

One of the best Sci-Fi movies out there. Great story and VFX.

Avengers - Infinity War

Amazing bgm, story, Vfx and action sequences. The theatre experience of this movie was 2nd to none.
